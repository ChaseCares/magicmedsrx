Steps to getting your brand name prescription medicine(s) FREE for the year:

  1. Find your Brand name medicine in the M.A.G.I.C. Guide under the PATIENTS link
  2. Register so we know you need help!
  3. Await a call from a M.A.G.I.C. MEDS representative to help you the rest of the way!
  4. ONLY if approved for FREE medicine for the year, pay a $100 annual fee

M.A.G.I.C. is intended for use by persons who have trouble affording ANY of their brand name medications. MAGIC includes programs offered for > 621 medications through the drug manufacturers where your medication is sent to your home or your provider's office for FREE for an entire year. Each program that a person may be eligible for requires the following information for eligibility:

  1. Annual Income
  2. Number of persons in household
  3. Insurance status
Prescription insurance coverage accepted:
  1. Medicare (A, B, D and Advantage plans)
  2. Uninsured
