Steps to getting your brand name prescription medicine(s) FREE for the year:

  1. Find your Brand name medicine in the M.A.G.I.C. Guide under the PATIENTS link
  2. Register so we know you need help!
  3. Await a call from a M.A.G.I.C. Meds representative to help you the rest of the way!
  4. ONLY if approved for FREE medicine for the year, pay a $100 annual fee


This assistance guide is intended for use by persons who have financial constraints related to ANY of their brand name medications. The programs are through the manufacturer of the drug and sent to your home or your provider's office for FREE for an entire year. Each program that a person may be eligible for requires the following information for eligibility:

  1. Annual Income
  2. Number of persons in household

Your insurance status must be one of the following to qualify:
  • Medicare (A,B,D and Advantage plans)
  • Uninsured
