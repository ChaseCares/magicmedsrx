How do I start getting free medicine through M.A.G.I.C. Meds Rx?


    Start by finding the brand name medication(s) you are having trouble affording through by clicking the image below:

  1. Then follow these steps:

  1. 1. Check to make sure you are under the income threshold for that medication(s) program eligibility

  2. 2. Register yourself on M.A.G.I.C. Meds Rx

  3. 3. Await phone call from M.A.G.I.C. Meds representative

  4. 4. Provide necessary income verification forms required by drug manufacturer program and sign application

  5. 5. Wait for M.A.G.I.C. Meds representative phone call to tell you whether or not you were approved!

What's the catch? There isn't one! You pay a $100 fee for processing and follow-through and you are only charged if you are approved for free medication!
